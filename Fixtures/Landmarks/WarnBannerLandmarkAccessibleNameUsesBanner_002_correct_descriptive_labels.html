Proper Landmark Labeling

When labels are needed to distinguish multiple landmarks, use descriptive terms that explain the purpose or content, not the landmark type.

Best Practice: If you need to label landmarks to distinguish multiple instances, use labels that describe the specific purpose or content (e.g., "Main site header", "Promotional announcement", "User dashboard header") rather than repeating the landmark type (e.g., "Banner", "Site banner").

Good vs. Bad Label Examples

Good labels:

  • aria-label="Main site header"
  • aria-label="Promotional announcement"
  • aria-label="User account header"

Avoid these labels:

  • aria-label="Banner" (redundant)
  • aria-label="Site banner" (redundant)
  • aria-label="Top banner section" (redundant)
