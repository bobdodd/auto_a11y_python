Note: This single contentinfo landmark doesn't require a label - this is the standard pattern.

Best Practice: Most pages should have exactly one contentinfo landmark (footer element), which doesn't need a label. If multiple contentinfo landmarks are necessary (very rare), provide each with a unique aria-label or aria-labelledby. However, consider whether complementary landmarks might be more appropriate for additional footer-like content.
