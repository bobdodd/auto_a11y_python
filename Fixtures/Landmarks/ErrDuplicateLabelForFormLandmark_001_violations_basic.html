Duplicate Form Landmark Labels - Violations

Understanding Form Landmark Labels

Form elements become landmarks when they have an accessible name (via aria-label or aria-labelledby). When multiple form landmarks exist, duplicate labels prevent screen reader users from distinguishing between them.
