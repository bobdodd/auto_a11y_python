Testing Instructions

To test high contrast mode:

  • Windows: Press Left Alt + Left Shift + Print Screen to toggle High Contrast mode
  • macOS: Go to System Preferences ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Display ‚Üí Increase Contrast
  • Browser DevTools: Emulate "prefers-contrast: high" in developer tools

Expected issues in high contrast mode:

  • Custom checkboxes may become invisible or unusable
  • Progress bars may lose visual indication
  • Status indicators (colored dots) may disappear
  • Subtle text may become unreadable
  • Icon buttons may lack sufficient visual boundaries
  • Tags/labels may lose background colors
  • Hero section text overlay may become invisible
  • Form focus indicators may not be visible
