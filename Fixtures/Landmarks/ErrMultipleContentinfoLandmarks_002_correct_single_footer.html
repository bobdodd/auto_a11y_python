Welcome to Our Website

This page follows the most common and recommended pattern: a single contentinfo landmark (footer) for site-wide footer information.

This provides a clear, unambiguous footer section that screen reader users can easily navigate to.

When to Use ContentInfo

The contentinfo landmark (footer element) should contain:

  • Copyright information
  • Legal links (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service)
  • Contact information
  • Site-wide footer navigation
  • Social media links
