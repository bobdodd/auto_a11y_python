Test: Outline Width with Em/Rem (Sufficient)

These buttons use em or rem units for outline-width that compute to at least 2px, meeting WCAG 2.4.11 requirements while maintaining responsiveness to font-size changes.

0.125em Width (Exactly 2px on 16px font)

0.125em on 16px font = 2px - meets minimum requirement.

0.15rem Width (2.4px on 16px root)

0.15rem on 16px root font-size = 2.4px - comfortable margin above minimum.

0.2em Width (3.2px - Generous)

0.2em on 16px font = 3.2px - generous width for excellent visibility.

Best Practice: Use 0.125em/rem minimum (= 2px on 16px font), or better yet 0.15-0.2em/rem for comfortable visibility across different font sizes.
