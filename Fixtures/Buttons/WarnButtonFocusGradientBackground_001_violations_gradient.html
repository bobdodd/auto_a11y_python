Test: Gradient Background Buttons

These buttons have gradient backgrounds, making it impossible to programmatically verify that focus outlines meet 3:1 contrast against all parts of the gradient. Manual verification is required.

Linear Gradient (Light to White)

Outline must be checked against both the lightest and darkest gradient colors.

Radial Gradient (Blue Shades)

Radial gradients have varying colors from center to edge.

Multi-Stop Gradient (Purple-Pink)

Complex gradients with multiple color stops need testing at each stop.
