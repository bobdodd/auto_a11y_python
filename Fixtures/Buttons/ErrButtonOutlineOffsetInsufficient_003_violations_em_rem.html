Test: Outline Offset with Em/Rem (Insufficient)

These buttons use em or rem units for outline-offset that compute to less than 2px. The outline sits too close to the button edge, getting lost within the button border or blending with the button itself.

0.05em Offset (0.8px - Too Close)

0.05em on 16px font = 0.8px - outline almost touches button edge.

0.1rem Offset (1.6px - Insufficient)

0.1rem on 16px root = 1.6px - insufficient separation from button.

0.11em Offset (1.76px - Still Insufficient)

0.11em on 16px font = 1.76px - close but still fails threshold.

Note: Use at least 0.125em or 0.125rem (= 2px on 16px font) for outline-offset to ensure clear visual separation.
