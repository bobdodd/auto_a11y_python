Test: Single-Side Box-Shadow Focus Indicators

These buttons use outline:none with box-shadow that only appears on one side or edge. This fails to provide a visible focus indicator from all directions, making focus ambiguous or invisible depending on viewing angle and surrounding elements.

Bottom Shadow Only

Shadow only appears on the bottom edge (y-offset = 4px).

Right Shadow Only

Shadow only appears on the right edge (x-offset = 4px).

Top-Left Shadow (Negative Offset)

Shadow appears on top-left corner only (negative x and y offsets).

Directional Shadow with Blur

Blurred shadow on bottom-right corner only (x=3px, y=3px with blur).
