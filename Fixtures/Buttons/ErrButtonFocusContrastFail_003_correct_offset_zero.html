Test: Outline with Zero Offset

When outline-offset is 0, the outline sits ON the button edge, so contrast should be measured against the BUTTON's background, not the parent's background.

Yellow Outline on Blue Button (offset: 0)

With outline-offset: 0px, the yellow (#ffff00) outline sits ON the button edge.
Contrast is measured against the button's blue background (#0066cc): ~10:1 (excellent!)
Parent background (#111) is irrelevant in this case.
