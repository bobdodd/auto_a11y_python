Accessibility Issue

The form fields above use a subtle background color change when focused (white to light blue), along with a border color change. However, there is no outline property.

Why this is problematic:

  • Users with screen magnification at 200% or higher may only see the text cursor inside the field, not the field boundaries or background
  • The background color change is very subtle (#ffffff to #f0f8ff) and may not be noticeable to users with color perception issues
  • Border color changes require viewing the entire field perimeter, which is difficult at high magnification
  • Users who look away and return to the form cannot quickly identify which field has focus
  • An outline property would provide a clear, persistent indicator at the field boundary that's visible even at high zoom
