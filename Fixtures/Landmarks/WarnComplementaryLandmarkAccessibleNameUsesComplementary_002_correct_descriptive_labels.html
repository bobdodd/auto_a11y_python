Best Practice: Use labels that describe what the complementary section contains (e.g., "Related articles", "Quick navigation", "Newsletter subscription") rather than using the generic term "complementary" or "complementary section".

Good vs. Bad Label Examples

Good labels:

  • aria-label="Related articles"
  • aria-label="Quick navigation"
  • aria-label="Newsletter subscription"
  • aria-label="Author biography"

Avoid these labels:

  • aria-label="Complementary" (redundant)
  • aria-label="Complementary content" (redundant)
  • aria-label="Complementary section" (redundant)
