Test: Outline Width with Em/Rem (Insufficient)

These buttons use em or rem units for outline-width that compute to less than 2px. Developers may assume these relative units are sufficient, but they fail to meet the WCAG 2.4.11 requirement of at least 2 CSS pixels.

0.1em Width (Computes to 1.6px on 16px font)

0.1em on 16px font = 1.6px - too thin.

0.1rem Width (Computes to 1.6px on 16px root)

0.1rem on 16px root font-size = 1.6px - too thin.

0.11em Width (Computes to 1.76px - Still Insufficient)

0.11em on 16px font = 1.76px - close but still fails threshold.

Note: To meet the 2px requirement, use at least 0.125em or 0.125rem (which equals exactly 2px on a 16px base font).
