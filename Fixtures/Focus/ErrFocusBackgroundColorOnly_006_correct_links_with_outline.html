Tab through the navigation links above. Each link has a clear orange outline when focused, in addition to the background color change.

Accessibility Features

  • Orange outline: High contrast against blue background (approximately 3:1 or better)
  • 2px outline offset: Creates clear space between link edge and focus indicator
  • Persistent marker: Outline remains visible when users return focus after looking away
  • Magnification-friendly: Outline is visible even at 400% zoom when users may only see part of the link
  • Dual indicators: Both outline and background color provide redundancy

The combination of outline (required) and background color change (supplementary) provides excellent focus visibility for all users, meeting and exceeding WCAG 2.4.7 requirements.
