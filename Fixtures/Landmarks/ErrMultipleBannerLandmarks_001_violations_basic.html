Understanding Banner Landmarks

Typically, a page should have only one banner landmark (the site header). If multiple banner landmarks exist without unique labels, screen reader users cannot distinguish between them.

Best practice: Use only one banner per page, or provide unique aria-label/aria-labelledby for each.
