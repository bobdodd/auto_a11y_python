WarnInputFocusGradientBackground - Warnings

This page contains text input fields with gradient backgrounds. Automated tools cannot reliably measure contrast across all gradient colors. Manual verification is required to ensure focus indicators maintain 3:1 contrast against ALL background colors in the gradient. All 4 inputs should generate warnings.

Gradient Background Inputs

Expected Test Results:

  • Warning Code: WarnInputFocusGradientBackground
  • Count: 4 warnings
  • WCAG: 2.4.7 Focus Visible (Level AA), 1.4.11 Non-text Contrast (Level AA)
  • Why: Automated tools cannot measure contrast against gradient backgrounds - contrast may be sufficient at one end but insufficient at the other
  • Action Required: Manually verify focus indicator has ≥3:1 contrast against ALL colors in the gradient
  • Recommendation: Use solid background colors when possible, or ensure focus indicators have very high contrast (e.g., black on light gradients, white on dark gradients)
  • Note: This is a limitation of automated testing, not necessarily a WCAG violation
