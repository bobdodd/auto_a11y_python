ErrTabindexFocusContrastFail - Violation Examples

This page contains elements with tabindex that have focus indicators with insufficient contrast (< 3:1 against background). All should generate errors.

Low Contrast Focus Indicators

Violation 1: Very Low Contrast Outline (1.5:1)

Light gray (#d0d0d0) outline on white background - nearly invisible

Violation 2: Low Contrast Box-Shadow (2.2:1)

Medium gray (#a8a8a8) shadow - still below 3:1 requirement

Violation 3: Subtle Border Change (1.8:1)

Border changes from #e0e0e0 to #b8b8b8 - not enough contrast

Violation 4: Transparent Low Contrast (2.5:1)

Semi-transparent gray outline - combined issues of transparency and low contrast

Violation 5: Colored But Low Contrast (2.8:1)

Very light blue box-shadow - color doesn't help if contrast is too low

Expected Test Results:

  • Count: 5 violations detected
  • WCAG: 2.4.7 Focus Visible (Level AA), 1.4.11 Non-text Contrast (Level AA)

    • Violation 1: ~1.5:1 (light gray outline)
    • Violation 2: ~2.2:1 (medium gray shadow)
    • Violation 3: ~1.8:1 (subtle border change)
    • Violation 4: ~2.5:1 (transparent + low contrast)
