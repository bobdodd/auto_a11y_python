Form Labeling Test Fixture - WarnFormHasNoLabel

This fixture tests various scenarios for form elements with and without accessible names to trigger the WarnFormHasNoLabel accessibility issue.

❌ Form with No Label (Primary Test Case)

FAIL - Should trigger warning

A basic form with no accessible name - the most common violation.

❌ Multiple Unlabeled Forms

FAIL - Should trigger multiple warnings

Multiple forms without labels - users can't distinguish their purposes.

❌ Form with Adjacent Heading (No aria-labelledby)

FAIL - Should trigger warning

Form has a relevant heading nearby but doesn't use aria-labelledby to connect them.
