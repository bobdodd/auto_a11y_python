Test: Outline Offset with Em/Rem (Sufficient)

These buttons use em or rem units for outline-offset that compute to at least 2px, ensuring clear visual separation between the button and its focus indicator.

0.125em Offset (Exactly 2px on 16px font)

0.125em on 16px font = 2px - meets minimum separation requirement.

0.15rem Offset (2.4px - Comfortable)

0.15rem on 16px root = 2.4px - comfortable separation.

0.25em Offset (4px - Generous)

0.25em on 16px font = 4px - generous separation for excellent visibility.

Best Practice: Use 0.125-0.15em/rem minimum for outline-offset, or 0.2-0.25em/rem for better visibility across different button styles and backgrounds.
